Fine recipe for delicious spaghetti bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1kg meat
  • tomatoes
  • carrots
  • onion
  • garlic
  • spaghetti noodles
  • salt
  • pepper

Preparations

  1. Rost the mear with some of garlic and onions
  2. Put in small cutted tomatoes, rest of garlic and onions
  3. Roast for another 10-15min
  4. Boil water for the spaghetti, boil the noodles for 5-7min
  5. Taste it, give in some salt and pepper - READY TO SERVE
